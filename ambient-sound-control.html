How It Works

  • Random Timing: Each sound plays at random intervals between 1-5 minutes
  • No Overlap: Only one ambient sound plays at a time
  • Automatic Start: Sounds begin when the game initializes
  • Staggered Delays: Initial sounds are staggered to prevent simultaneous playing
  • Volume Control: Wind sounds at 30%, chimes at 40% volume
